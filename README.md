## 📜 JUTSU LIST (FEATURES)

### 🔴 Taijutsu (Group Management)
*   **`.kick`** - Banish a member from the village.
*   **`.promote`** - Grant Ninja Rank (Admin).
*   **`.demote`** - Revoke Ninja Rank.
*   **`.tagall`** - Call all Shinobi to battle.

### 🔵 Genjutsu (Entertainment & Games)
*   **`.gamble`** - Risk your Ryo (Money) in the casino.
*   **`.work`** - Complete D-Rank missions for coins.
*   **`.coinflip`** - Test your luck with a flip.
*   **`.anime`** - Summon random high-quality anime art.

### 🟡 Fuinjutsu (Utilities)
*   **`.owner`** - Reveal the creator of this Jutsu.
*   **`.help`** - Open the Scroll of Commands.
*   **`.ping`** - Check the bot's reaction speed.
